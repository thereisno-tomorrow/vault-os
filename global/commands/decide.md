Capture the current operational decision to `ops/decisions.md`.

Append an entry in this format:

```
## YYYY-MM-DD — [Decision title]
[What was decided and why. Include what was ruled out and the reason. Write enough that the next session doesn't re-litigate from scratch.]
```

Run this at the moment a decision is made — not at session end. Nuance is lost by the end of the session.
